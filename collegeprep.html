GRE/GMAT

GRE (Graduate Record Examination): The GRE is a standardized test used for admissions to graduate and business programs worldwide. It assesses skills in verbal reasoning, quantitative reasoning, and analytical writing.

SOP

An SOP is a concise essay required as part of a university or job application. It outlines a candidate's goals, qualifications, and reasons for pursuing a specific program or position. It helps selection committees assess an applicant's suitability and motivations.

IELTS/TOEFL

IELTS (International English Language Testing System): IELTS is a widely recognized English language proficiency test. It assesses the language skills of non-native English speakers who wish to study, work, or immigrate in English-speaking countries. IELTS evaluates listening, reading, writing, and speaking abilities.

SAT

The SAT is a standardized test commonly used for college admissions in the United States. It assesses a student's readiness for college and is intended to provide colleges with a common data point that can be used to compare all applicants. The test measures skills in math, evidence-based reading, and writing. SAT scores are an important factor in the college application process.
